ZigZag

offers a fresh take the layout of a ranked grid.


This here is a heatmap, the greener the color the closer the cell is to the top left. In western cultures our eye is drawn to the top left. So how do we ensure that the best content is in your glance?


Traditionally we use a left to right, top to bottom approach to laying out a ranked grid (blue). We could sort by distance from the top-left (red). ZigZag offers the best of both worlds by clustering top content in the left corner and settling into the familar left to right pattern (purple).
